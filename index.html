Project Mitotic Wave

Investigated mitotic wave synchronization in starfish embryos using light-sheet microscopy, capturing 10+ hour time-lapse videos of embryogenesis from 32-cell to 2000-cell stages with fluorescent membrane staining. Developed image analysis pipelines for nucleus detection and tracking using morphological operations (top-hat transform, Otsu thresholding) and TrackMate software to extract spatiotemporal patterns of coordinated cell divisions across the developing embryo.
